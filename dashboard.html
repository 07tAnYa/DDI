Quick facts

  • High severity: avoid combination or consult specialist.
  • Moderate: monitor, dose adjustment may be required.
  • Low: typically manageable with monitoring.

Glossary

CYP450
Liver enzymes that metabolize many drugs; interactions often involve CYP induction/inhibition.
QT prolongation
A heart rhythm effect that can be dangerous when drugs combine.
Serotonin syndrome
Life-threatening agitation, hyperthermia, autonomic instability — occurs with serotonergic drugs.
